The Coasts of California

Santa Monica, LA

Santa Monica is a city on the South Coast of California. It is famous for its resort towns with a climate enjoying an average of 310 days of sunshine per year. As of 2020, it has a population of 93,076. Notable restaurants in the area include Madame Wu's Garden, Batterfish, Stout Burgers and Beers, and The Misfit.

Oxnard

Oxnard is a city in Ventura County with a population of 202,063 as of 2020. It is at the western edge of Oxnard Plain adjacent to agricultural fields with strawberries, lima beans, and other vegetable crops. Oxnard has important biological communities such as the coastal sage scrub. It is home to the endangered Ventura Marsh Milkvetch. It enjoys an annual 300 days of sunshine.

Ventura

Ventura is a city in the county seat of Ventura County, California. As of 2020, it had a population of 110,763. It is a popular tourist destination due to its historic landmarks, beaches, and resorts. Located along the coast of California, northwest of LA, Ventura enjoys a mediterranean climate. Patagonia is based in Ventura.
