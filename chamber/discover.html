Demographics

Population

Within the municipalities in Metro Manila, Makati ranks 9th with a population of 629 616 as of the 2020 census.

Geography

Makati is located at the center of Metro Manila bounded by Pasig River and the cities of Pateros, Taguig, Manila, and Pasay. It has a total land area of 27.36 square kilometers.

Climate

The city has a tropical monsoon climate based on the Koppen climate classificaiton system. Temperature ranges between 20¬∞C and 38¬∞C with usually high humidity levels making the area feel warmer.

Financial Center

As the financial center of the Philippines, Makati boasts the highest concentration of corporations in the country whether multinational or local.

Cultural and Entertainment Hub

Fine art museums, colonial-era churches and recreation areas dot the city of Makati. The Manila Golf Club, Manila Polo Club, and Makati Coliseum are some of the famouse sports landmark in the city.

Shopping and Lifestyle

Various shopping centers offer high-end lifestyle for the rich and the famous. Some of the famous shopping centers located in Makati are Greenbelt and Power Plant Mall.

Education

The Makati Government is well-known for its support in the education sector. Its public schools, well-cared for, many of its citizens, also known as Makatizens, are highly educated people. Some of the institutions that have branched in the city include Ateneo de Manila University, De La Salle University, Far Eastern University, and Centro Escolar University.

Makati City Hall

The Makati City Hall located along Angono Street is a one-stop hub for business and government transactions. This provides convenience for its citizens.
