Site Name

The site will be named, "Makati Chamber of Commerce".

It will be hosted on the following domain.

https://makaticitychamber.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote the businesses of the members of the Makati Chamber of Commerce. It also aims to promote the area of Makati, and attract more businesses, people, and tourism. This page would serve as an avenue for business owners in Makati to see who's who and what they're up to. It would also help keep the members up to date with the latest events or meetings held by the organization.
