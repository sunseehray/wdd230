Overview

The target audience is business owners from Makati. These include sole proprietorships, partnerships, and corporations. They are mainly tech-savvy people who know how to navigate the internet and connect with people online. They want to know what's going on in terms of commerce in the area of Makati.

Personas

Nina, Restaurateur

Nina is the owner of a restaurant in Makati. She is married with kids and in her mid-40s, and she has been running her business for 15 years. Nina promotes her business through social media and word of mouth. She has a bachelor's degree in hotel and restaurant management.

Nina would access the site through her work laptop in her office during off-peak business hours.

James, Vegetables & Fruits Retailer

James is a young, single entrepreneur currently taking up his bachelor's in business administration. He has a small business selling vegetables and fruits at a public market. He is thinking of expanding his business by becoming a supplier in the food service industry.

James would access the site through his mobile phone at home after a day's work.

Scenarios

Are there any upcoming organization events that I should know about?

Nina is wondering if the chamber is hosting any events soon and she would like to get updated on its details.

How's the economy doing these days?

Nina would like to read some insightful articles from the page to see how businesses are doing at present. She wants to know if there are some issues she has to look out for that might be a problem or an opportunity for her business.

How are some of the businesses doing these days?

James is curious about what businesses are out there that he can potentially form a partnership with. He scrolls through the news articles and sees some businesses suffering from inadequate supply.

How do I register as a member of the chamber of commerce?

James wants to know what are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber and how he can register.

How do I reach out to someone in this organization?

James has some questions for the Chamber and would like to know how to contact them.
