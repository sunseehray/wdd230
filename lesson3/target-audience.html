Target Audience

The target audience are business owners whether micro, small, or big. These are Filipino adults, 18 years old and above, who have or an interested in doing business in Makati. They are mainly tech-savvy people who know how to navigate the internet and connect with people online.

Personas

One persona would be a micro-business owner who is interested in connecting with potential partners for their business such as suppliers, marketers, etc. They would also be interested in learning about events in order to promote their business to the public. When they visit the page, they would like to know about announcements from the Chamber and insights about the economy. Another persona would be B2B business owners. They would be interested in getting to know other businesses in the area and how they can supply their services to them. They would use the web page to promote their services to others. They would also visit the page to see how other businesses are doing.

Scenarios

Micro Business Owner looking for event announcements

Micro Business Owner wanting to know how the economy looks like

B2B owner wanting to discover what other businesses are out there

B2B owner wanting to register to the chamber

An individual looking to discover what businesses are up to
